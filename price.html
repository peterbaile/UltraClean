Frequently Asked Questions

See if you have the same concerns about the Ultrasound Washing Machine.

Is the ultrasound washing machine noisy?
This can't be the case. Ultrasound cannot be detected by human ears.
Can the washing machine remove bad smell?
Yes. Smell can be detected due to the precense of particular molecules. Since smell is still particles, ultrasound can break that away.
Is it we just have to add water to the tank?
Yes, you are right. You don't even have to use hot water. Simply cold tap water will do. However, you must have something inside the tank because air is a poor medium for transmitting waves.
Will clothes be damaged?
I don't think so. After washing the same piece of clothes for 10 months, we see very limited differences before and after.
Is it we must hang the clothes on the bar?
At the current stage, yes. If the clothes just crumble together, the ultrasound waves is unlikely to pass through every piece of clothes, thereby leading to inefficiency. However, we are working on ways to improve this.
